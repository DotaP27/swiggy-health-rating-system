# Demo Script — 60–90s walkthrough

Start: 0–5s — Title screen (app name)

5–20s — Problem statement
- "When shopping quickly, nutrition labels are hard to parse. Instamart Health Rater shows a simple grade next to each product so customers can choose healthier options at a glance."

20–50s — Show the demo UI
- Open the demo homepage, hover over the grade chip to reveal the numeric score and top factors. Click a product to show a compact breakdown.

50–80s — Explain integration
- "The scoring module maps Instamart nutrition fields into a per-100g normalization, applies weights, and adjusts with ingredient cues. It runs in the browser and requires no backend. After Builders Club approval, the app will fetch live product data and show chips in product lists."

80–90s — Call to action
- "I'm ready to integrate with Builders Club APIs — please provide sandbox credentials and sample schema."
